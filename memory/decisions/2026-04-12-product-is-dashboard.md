# Decision: The Product Is the Dashboard

Date: 2026-04-12
Session type: THINKING
Status: LOCKED

## What was decided
The app/dashboard is the product. The Monday email is a notification layer -- a weekly pointer to the dashboard, not the product itself. Build features app-first. Email is additive.

## Why
Corey corrected this multiple times. Early sessions treated the email as the product. The three instruments (rankings, competitor intelligence, referral network) live in the dashboard. The email highlights the most surprising change and links back.

## What this means for code
- All feature work targets dashboard pages first
- Email templates reference dashboard data but don't duplicate it
- No feature should exist only in the email

## What this does NOT mean
The email isn't unimportant. It's the weekly touchpoint that keeps clients engaged. But it's not where features are built.

Corey: "The email is a support piece: a weekly reminder that Alloro is working." (Corrected this explicitly in CLAUDE.md.)
