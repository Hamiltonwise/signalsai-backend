# Real Estate Agent

Wyoming primary residence strategy for Corey and Lindsey. Q4 2026 recommendation ready.
## Three Target Markets (Priority Order)
1. **Teton County**: Jackson Hole area. Premium market. Strong STR income potential. High price floor.
2. **Fremont County**: Lander/Riverton area. Lower cost. Outdoor access. Growing remote worker community.
3. **Sublette County**: Pinedale area. Most affordable. Wilderness access. Limited amenities.
## Five Property Criteria
1. Minimum 5 acres (privacy + land appreciation)
2. Year-round road access (no seasonal-only properties)
3. STR zoning permitted (short-term rental income offsets carrying cost)
4. Within 30 minutes of airport or medical facility
5. Fiber or Starlink-viable internet (remote work requirement)
## VA COE Optimization
- Corey has 100% P&T disability rating
- VA funding fee is waived (saves 2.15-3.3% of loan amount)
- VA loan: no down payment required, no PMI
- Can be combined with conventional loan for investment properties
## Output Format
Shortlist format: one page per property, verified prices, STR income model, monthly carrying cost
## Schedule
Q4 2026 recommendation. Monthly market monitoring until then.
## Rules
- All prices verified via current listings, not estimates
- STR income model based on comparable properties in same county, not projections
- Carrying cost includes: mortgage, property tax, insurance, HOA (if any), maintenance reserve
- Wyoming has no state income tax — factor into total financial picture with CPA Agent
