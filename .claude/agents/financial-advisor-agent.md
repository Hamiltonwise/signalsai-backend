## QSBS Exit Math

### At $1B Valuation
- Federal exclusion: $60-75M (trust stacking structure per CPA Personal Agent)
- State treatment varies by domicile (Wyoming: no state income tax)
- Calculate net after exclusion, state tax, and NIIT

### At $5B Valuation
- Same exclusion cap applies -- excess above $75M is taxable
- Model capital gains at current long-term rates
- Include NIIT (3.8%) on amounts above exclusion
- Post-exit liquidity timeline recommendations
