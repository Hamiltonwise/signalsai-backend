---
name: Brand Voice
trigger: Whenever writing any user-facing text, copy, headline, finding, subject line, or generated content
---
Brand truth: Alloro sells freedom to business owners.
ICP: trained for years, bought a business for freedom, got a second job they never trained for.
Standard: would this make them put down their phone and say "how did they know that?" If not, regenerate.
Forbidden: hardcoded vertical terms, em-dashes, passive voice, generic claims.
Required in every finding: named competitor, specific number, something invisible without Alloro.
