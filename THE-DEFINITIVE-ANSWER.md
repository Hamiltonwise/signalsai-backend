## RESOLUTION: 5 Pages vs 3 Instruments

### The tension

Product Constitution (April 4): Home, Compare, Reviews, Presence, Progress -- five pages justified by Cowan (2001) cognitive chunking (4 plus/minus 1).

8 Questions (April 7-8): Three instruments -- Can people find you? Do people trust you? Is the business producing?

### The resolution

They're not in conflict. They're at different altitudes.

**The 3 instruments are the QUESTIONS the business owner carries.** They're the mental model. They map to what the owner actually worries about:

1. "Can people find me?" = Visibility (Maps, rankings, presence)
2. "Do people trust me when they find me?" = Reputation (Reviews, ratings, responses)
3. "Is my business producing?" = Production (Referrals, revenue, operational health)

**The 5 pages are VIEWS within those instruments.** They're the implementation detail:

| Instrument | Pages within it |
|---|---|
| Can people find you? | Compare (market position), Presence (GBP completeness, directories) |
| Do people trust you? | Reviews (what people say, response rate, sentiment) |
| Is the business producing? | Home (the summary), Progress (trends over time) |

**But the 8 Questions already resolved this further.** The instruments aren't pages at all. They're sections of the Monday email. The email IS the product (Known 8). The dashboard is the reference library you visit when you want to go deeper.

So the correct architecture is:

**Monday email = 3 instruments, delivered.**
Each instrument gets a reading: a number, a context line, a verify link.

**Dashboard = the reference library.**
Organized by the same 3 instruments, with drill-down depth for when the owner wants to pull (not push) information.

The 5-page structure from the Constitution was correct at the time. The 3-instrument model from the 8 Questions is the evolution. The evolution didn't invalidate the pages -- it reorganized them under a simpler mental model.

**The Constitution should be updated to reflect this.** The cognitive science still applies -- Cowan's 4+/-1 now maps to 3 instruments (well within the range), not 5 pages.

Organized by the 3 instruments. The owner visits when they want to pull information. Progressive disclosure: calm surface, depth when you pull. Never forces interpretation. Never shows a chart without a sentence explaining what it means.

The dashboard's role is NOT to deliver value. The email delivers value. The dashboard proves the email is true. Every number in the email has a corresponding deep-dive in the dashboard that shows the work behind it.
