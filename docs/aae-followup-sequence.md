## Sequence Rules

1. **Stop the sequence immediately** if they create an account. Move to the onboarding sequence.
2. **Personalize** the city, specialty, and region from the scan data captured at the booth.
3. **Never send more than one email per day.** Respect the inbox.
4. **Every subject line is about them, not about Alloro.** No product announcements. No feature lists. Only findings and curiosity.
5. **Plain text wins.** No heavy branding. These should feel like a person wrote them, because a person did.
6. **Track opens and clicks per email.** If Email 1 gets no open, resend with alternate subject on Day 2: "We found your top competitor at AAE."
