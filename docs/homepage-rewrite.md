## DESIGN NOTES

- Above the fold: ONLY the headline, subhead, and search input. Nothing else. No nav clutter. No feature icons. Her story, the promise, and the box.
- Every section starts with HER, not with Alloro. "You took the leap." "You spent years." "You're not failing."
- The Monday email section shows a REAL email preview, not a description. She sees the actual subject line and body.
- No feature lists anywhere. No bullet points of capabilities. Each section tells one story.
- The price section leads with what she gets for FREE before showing the number.
- The Foundation section is not at the bottom. It's before the price. She learns about the community before she learns about the cost. That changes what $2,000 means.
- The "Who Built This" section does not name Corey directly (per his request not to make it about him). It lets the story speak.
- Mobile-first. Every line readable at 390px without squinting.
- Colors: warm backgrounds (cream, soft terracotta wash), navy text, terracotta CTA buttons. No cold whites. No corporate blues.
- Zero jargon. Zero acronyms. Zero "AI-powered." Zero "platform." Zero "solution."
