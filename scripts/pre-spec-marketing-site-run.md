## 3. ENTRY POINTS
- [x] Discovery channels: Google search, AI citation, referral, competitor trigger, badge click, QR code, social share, market report, partner channel, outbound (ProspectAI)
- [x] Emotional state mapping:
  - Search: skeptical, comparing. Show proof + differentiation.
  - Referral: pre-warmed, trusting. Show checkup directly.
  - Competitor trigger: anxious, competitive. Show urgency.
  - Badge: curious, casual. Show score concept.
  - QR (AAE): conference mode, 30-second attention. Show one thing.
  - AI citation: researching, high intent. Show data + authority.
  - Market report: researching specific market. Show local data.
- [x] Programmatic: 3,400 micropages (200 cities x 17 verticals) auto-generated from market data
- [x] AEO: Question-answer format, structured data, specific current numbers, entity-based
- NOTED: Missing entry point for SOCIAL MEDIA. If Corey posts a clip on Instagram or LinkedIn, where does that traffic land? Need a social-specific landing variant that's visual-first, mobile-optimized, and matches the tone of social content. ADDING.
