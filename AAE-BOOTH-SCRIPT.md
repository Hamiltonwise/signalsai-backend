# AAE Booth Script

Corey's playbook for the Alloro booth at AAE 2026. Booth #835.
Rehearse this 10 times before April 14.

## The Setup (before the show opens)

- Phone: sandbox.getalloro.com/demo loaded, logged in
- iPad: sandbox.getalloro.com/aae loaded (QR code destination)
- QR code printed: links to getalloro.com/aae
- WiFi backup: personal hotspot on, tested
- Demo login verified: demo@getalloro.com / demo2026

## The 90-Second Booth Flow

A doctor walks up. You have 90 seconds before they decide
if this is worth their time.

### Beat 1: The Hook (5 seconds)

Don't pitch. Ask.

"Do you know your rank in your market right now?
Like, the actual number?"

Most will say no. Some will say "I think so."
Either answer works.

### Beat 2: The Demo (30 seconds)

Open the demo dashboard on your phone. Show them.

"This is Valley Endodontics. They've been on Alloro for 90 days.
Watch what it does."

Point at the screen:

- "Week 8 of consecutive growth." (streak badge)
- "Dr. Rodriguez went quiet for 47 days. They called her.
  She came back. $18,000 protected." (win card)
- "12 reviews from passing their top competitor." (near-miss)

Don't explain the product. Let the data speak.

### Beat 3: The Pivot (10 seconds)

"That's 90 days. Want to see where you stand right now?"

Hand them the iPad or point at the QR code.

### Beat 4: The Checkup (60 seconds)

They type their name. The scanning theater runs.
Named competitors drop onto the map.

While it scans, say ONE thing:

"See those pins? Those are every endodontist in your market.
Named. Real review counts. You've probably never seen them
all on a map before."

The score appears. The sub-scores break down.
The blur gate shows a named competitor CTA.

### Beat 5: The Close (5 seconds)

"Check your email Monday at 7am. That's the product."

Don't ask for anything. Don't sell. Walk away if there's
a line. The Monday email does the selling.

## If They Ask About Price

"$2,000 a month. Everything included. No contracts.
Veterans and first responders: free. First-year businesses: $400.
But don't worry about that. Check your email Monday.
If it tells you something you didn't know, we'll talk."

## If They Ask How It Works

"47 AI agents watch your market around the clock. Every Monday
at 7am, one email. One thing that's specific, true, and
actionable. The catch before it costs you."

Don't go deeper. Complexity kills booth conversations.

## If They're Skeptical

"I get it. You've heard this before. Here's the difference:
I'm not asking you to sign up, connect anything, or talk to
sales. Just check your email Monday. If it's generic, delete
it. If it tells you something specific about your market
that you didn't know, that's the product."

## If They're a Veteran or First Responder

"Alloro is free for veterans and first responders. Permanently.
Not a trial. Not discounted. Free. I served in the Air Force.
I built the Foundation because the people who served our
communities deserve the intelligence everyone else pays for."

Hand them a card. Point them to the Foundation page.

## If They Want to Talk More

"I'd love that. Here's my card. But honestly, the best
thing you can do right now is run the checkup and see
your actual data. Everything else follows from that."

## The One Rule

Never lead with the product. Lead with their data.
The product earns attention by performing, not by being described.
